<\!-- 7: DESIGN NOTES -->

Why these changes?

🇳🇬 Nigerian Voice

Pidgin English copy ("no wahala", "wetin happen") feels familiar and builds instant trust with Nigerian users.

🟢 Green, Not Red

Green = money = go. We lead with emerald for safety cues. Red only appears for genuine problems.

🔤 Larger Type

Hero text at 36px+. Critical info (amounts, codes) never below 16px. Readability on bright Lagos sunlight.

☝️ One Primary Action

Every screen has exactly ONE dominant CTA. Reduces cognitive load and speeds completion.

🛡️ Trust Signals First

CBN, NDIC, NDPR badges appear before any CTA. Vault metaphor in deal rooms. Bank partner always visible.

📴 Offline-First

USSD codes, SMS fallbacks, agent finder. 40% of target users are on 2G or intermittent data.

⚡ Fast Dispute Path

Reassurance FIRST, then problem selection. "Human not bot" promise. 24h SLA prominently displayed.

🔍 Scannable Deal States

4-stage progress with color-coded dots. Urgent deals get red edge-bar. Vault card shows exact amount held.

👑 Crown Jewel Differentiators

  • Vault-metaphor Deal Room with bank partner transparency
  • Trinity (BVN + NIN + Face) verification in Pidgin English
  • USSD-first offline payment — hero code, agent finder fallback
  • Dispute flow that reassures before it interrogates
  • WhatsApp-style chat embedded in every deal
  • Trust Score & escrow balance on home — safety at a glance
  • Pidgin copy throughout — "no wahala" as brand signature
  • One primary CTA per screen — zero decision fatigue
